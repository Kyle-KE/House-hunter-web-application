Frequently Asked Questions

What is house hunter?

House hunter is a service that makes it easier for students to look for accommodation around campus and give a breakdown of amenities, room types, prices etc for each hostel.

Where are you located?

House Hunter is purely based online

How can I contact a hostel if I want to book?

When you visit the hostel section and browse through it, the contact for the hostel is available in the hostels descripton.

If I book via house hunter, will I get a discount?

No, House hunter only serves as bridge between hostels and students and just provides information about the hostel. If you would like to know more about discounts, you can contact the hostel directly.

Are the hostels you are listing safe for students?

Yes! All the hostels listed in the hostel section are safe for student accommodation and have been verified by USIU-A.

Is house hunter only for students looking for hostels?

At the moment, house hunter is only providing information to students looking for hostels.

What happens if a hostel does not get back to me?

If a hostel does not get back to you in good time, you can plan to vist the hostel physically. In order to access the hostel physically, the directions for each and every hostel has been provided.
